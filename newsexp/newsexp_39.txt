Congress leaders observe 'Maun Vrat' in protest against Lakhimpur Kheri violence
NEW DELHI/PATNA: Congress leaders are observing a 'Maun Vrat' (vow of silence) across the country as a protest against the Lakhimpur Kheri violence. They are also demanding the dismissal of the Minister of State for Home Affairs Ajay Mishra whose son was arrested in connection with the incident. The Congress had earlier instructed state chiefs to carry out a protest in the form of a satyagraha.
