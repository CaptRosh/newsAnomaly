Minister's Son Arrested In Farmers' Killing Has Dengue, Taken To Hospital
Lucknow: Union Minister Ajay Mishra's son, Ashish Mishra, arrested for his alleged role in the Lakhimpur Kheri violence in Uttar Pradesh, is dengue positive and is being moved to the district hospital for treatment.Ashish Mishra, who was remanded in police custody on Friday, was moved to the Lakhimpur jail late last night after test results confirmed that he had contracted dengue. Also, he has diabetes," said Shailendra Bhatnagar, CMO, Lakhimpur Kheri.Four farmers and a journalist were run over on October 3 by a convoy of three vehicles, one of which belongs to Union Minister of state for home, Ajay Mishra. The minister's son was arrested on October 9, five days after he was named in the murder related to the incident on charges which usually merit immediate arrest.The families of farmers who died alleged in their complaint to the police that the minster's son was in the driving seat of the SUV that crushed the farmers.
