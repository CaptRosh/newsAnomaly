"Anti-Incumbency Against BJP In UP", Says Chhattisgarh Chief Minister
"Everyone is upset with Yogi Adityanath," Chhattisgarh Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel said. From farmers, youth, Scheduled communities to traders, everyone is upset with the CM.
