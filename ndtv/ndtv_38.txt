Priyanka Gandhi In UP's Lakhimpur Kheri To Pay Tribute To Farmers Killed

While some of the banners mentioned who had put them up, others appeared to have vague details about this aspect. All banners took digs at the Congress, saying 'people' don't want "fake sympathy".The banners proclaiming farmers don't want "fake sympathy"Preparations for the final prayers were made at a field, away from the spot where the violence took place in Tikonia village of Lakhimpur Kehri in poll-bound Uttar Pradesh.Ashish Mishra - son of Union Minister of State for home, Ajay Mishra - now arrested, is accused of running his SUV over slogan-shouting farmers in the district on October 3.
