15,786 Fresh Covid-19 Cases In India, 14% Lower Than Yesterday
India's COVID-19 recovery rate is currently at 98.16 per cent, highest since March 2020New Delhi: India reported 15,786 fresh COVID-19 cases in the last 24 hours, 14 per cent lower than yesterday's 18,454 cases.
