Soldier Killed In Action, 2 Terrorists Shot Dead In J&K Encounter
2 Lashkar-e-Taiba or LeT terrorists were killed in an encounter in J&K's Shopian district, said the IGPSrinagar: One Indian Army soldier was killed and two others were injured during an encounter with terrorists in Shopian area of south Kashmir today.
