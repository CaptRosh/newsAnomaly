Amit Shah Meets Family Of J&K Police Officer Killed By Terrorists
Amit Shah gave documents of appointment to a government job to the police officer's widow.Srinagar: Home Minister Amit Shah on Saturday visited the family of Jammu and Kashmir Police officer Parvaiz Ahmad who was killed by terrorists in June this year in Nowgam area of the city.Mr Shah, who arrived in Srinagar on a three-day visit to Jammu and Kashmir, drove straight to Nowgam from the airport."The home minister visited the house of Jammu and Kashmir Police, Inspector Parvaiz, who was killed by terrorists," a home ministry official said.Officials said Mr Shah offered his condolences to the family of the police officer.He also gave documents of appointment to a government job to the police officer's widow, Fatima Akhtar, on compassionate grounds.In a tweet later, Mr Shah said, "Today visited the family of martyr Parvaiz Ahmad Dar and paid tributes to him. I and the nation are proud of his bravery.
