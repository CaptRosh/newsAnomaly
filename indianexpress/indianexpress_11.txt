AP Inter Supplementary exams 2021 result declared: How to check scorecard
The Board of Intermediate Education, Andhra Pradesh (BIEAP) has declared the results for intermediate supplementary exams on its official website at bie.ap.gov.in. Candidates who had appeared for the supplementary exam can check their results using their registration number and password. The board has declared the results of both first-year and second-year students. The result has been declared for both vocational and regular students. This year, as many as 3,24,800 students from general and vocational courses appeared for the 1st year exam. For the second year, 14,950 students appeared for the AP inter supplementary exam 2021.
