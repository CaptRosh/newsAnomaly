Punjab agriculture minister hands over cheques to Lakhimpur victims
Punjab Agriculture Minister Randeep Singh Nabha on Friday handed over cheques of Rs 50 lakh each to the families of deceased farmers and a journalist, who were killed in the tragic incident at Lakhimpur Kheri earlier this month. Nabha met these aggrieved families at Lucknow and handed over the cheques. These four deceased farmers were Daljit Singh, Gurwinder Singh, Lovepreet Singh and Nachhatar Singh, besides a journalist Raman Kashyap.
